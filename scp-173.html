SCP-173: The Sculpture

SCP-173, also known as "The Sculpture," is a Euclid-class anomalous object that is considered to be extremely dangerous by the Foundation. It is a concrete statue that appears to be made of concrete and rebar. SCP-173 has the ability to move quickly and unpredictably, making it extremely difficult to contain and control. Despite its seemingly inanimate appearance, SCP-173 is capable of attacking and killing individuals who come into direct contact with it.

The Foundation first became aware of SCP-173 after reports of individuals disappearing in a specific location. After investigation, the Foundation discovered SCP-173 and immediately took measures to contain it. The exact origins of SCP-173 are unknown, but it is believed to have been created by a sculptor who had anomalous properties themselves.

SCP-173 is to be kept in a secure containment chamber at all times and monitored by at least two armed guards. Access to SCP-173's containment chamber is strictly limited and must be approved by Level 3 personnel or higher. Any individuals who come into direct contact with SCP-173 must be immediately neutralized to prevent the spread of its anomalous properties.

In conclusion, SCP-173 is a highly unpredictable and dangerous anomaly that requires constant vigilance and caution to prevent loss of life and containment breaches. The Foundation will continue to study SCP-173 and explore methods to further understand and control its abilities.
