Who is squid girl?

Squid Girl, a character, from the manga and anime series called "Shinryaku! Ika Musume" (which translates to "Invasion! Squid Girl") is a squid who emerges from the depths of the sea with grand ambitions of conquering humanity. However fate takes a turn as she finds herself working tirelessly at a restaurant earnestly trying to make amends for the havoc she unintentionally caused. One cannot. Notice Squid Girls appearance, featuring a squid like mantle adorning her head and hair that resembles tentacles. Her vibrant personality radiates with energy, mischievous antics and an unwavering longing to forge connections with humankind. Over time Squid Girl has garnered a following, among enthusiasts of anime and manga alike. Her captivating presence extends beyond the screen into forms of merchandise, video games and other media platforms. In Japan she is also affectionately known as Ika Musume.

Some activities to do on Squid Girl day

-Try to make squid puns at every chance possible for the entire day.

-Watch an episode of "Shinryaku! Ika Musume"

-Dress up as Squid Girl or another character from the series and take photos to share on social media.

-Host a Squid Girl-themed party with food and decorations inspired by the series, such as squid-themed snacks and drinks (Please read section 1a)

-Donate to a charity or cause that supports marine life conservation or environmental awareness, which are themes that are present in the series.

-Write fanfiction or fan comics featuring Squid Girl and her adventures.

-Create fan art of Squid Girl and share it with other fans online.

-Organize a beach clean-up event in honour of Squid Girl's love for the ocean.

-Host a watch party: Gather with friends and family to watch some of the most popular Squid Girl episodes or movies together.

-Go to the beach: Squid Girl spends a lot of time at the beach in the series, so why not celebrate Squid Girl Day by going to the beach with friends and family?

-Visit a local aquarium: Squid Girl is a squid, so why not spend the day learning more about real-life squid and other sea creatures at an aquarium? You could even hold a scavenger hunt to find items related to Squid Girl and the "Shinryaku! Ika Musume" series.

-Eat shrimp

-Spread awareness about the Squid Girl series by sharing information and links to the anime and manga with others who might be interested.
