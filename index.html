About SCP Central

SCP Central is a website dedicated to providing information and resources on the SCP Foundation and its contained entities, known as SCPs.

Our goal is to make it easy for people to learn about and find information on these mysterious and intriguing entities. Our site features a comprehensive database of SCPs, categorized by object class for easy navigation. Whether you're a fan of the SCP Foundation or simply curious about these strange and fascinating entities, SCP Central is the place for you.
